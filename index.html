Lusiana Ampeva is a contemporary visual artist, mostly focused on painting. Her artistic approach consists of rapid gestural movements, with free compositions, followed by an expressionistic, metaphysical and illustrative treatment. In her work she uses combinations of different materials, including oil, acrylic, sprays, gypsum, etc.

Artist's inspiration

The author often draws inspiration from the depth of her consciousness, which allows her to voluntarily come to the surface, layering all possible connections of awareness and unconsciousness to coexist in that space, on that canvas. Her paintings depict on parts of her personal life, environment, everyday spontaneous interactions with the world around her.
