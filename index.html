Lusiana's inspiration

Lusiana Ampeva is a contemporary visual artist, mostly focused on painting. Her artistic approach consists of rapid gestural movments, with free compositions, followed by an expressionistic, metaphysical and illustrative treatment. In her work she uses combinations of different materials, including oil, acrylic, sprays, gypsum, etc.
